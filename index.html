air plane

The Boing B737 is small jet aircraft made by Boing. It is the best-selling and longest-selling series of jet airliners in history and one of the best-selling and longest-selling commercial transport aircraft in the history of commercial transport aircraft.

The Boing B777 is Large wide-body twin-engine jet aircraft. It generally called "Triple 7". It is the world's largest twin-engine aircraft. Its diameter is as large as the fuselage of a 737, and it is equipped with powerful jet engines.

The Boeing B787 is a next-generation, long-range, medium-sized wide-body aircraft. It is a twin-engine aircraft equipped with two turbofan engines, one on each wing. It's called "Dreamliner.

The Airbus A320 is a single-aisle, twin-engine jet airliner developed and manufactured by the European Airbus. The A320 competed with Boeing worldwide and increased market share, making Airbus one of the two largest aircraft manufacturers in the world.

The airbus A350 is new generation medium to large wide-body aircraft developed and manufactured by Airbus; Qatar Airways commenced the world's first commercial flight on the Doha - Frankfurt route on January 15, 2015.

The Airbus A380 is an ultra-large wide-body aircraft equipped with four turbofan engines. It is also the world's first full-deck passenger aircraft and the world's largest passenger aircraft (in terms of passengers).

ATR 72 is a twin-engine turboprop airliner manufactured by ATR, a joint venture between French and Italian aircraft manufacturers.Nearly 30 years have passed since its first flight, but development continues to this day.

The Bombardier CRJ, also known as the CRJ family or CRJ series, is a family of twin-engine jet airliners manufactured and marketed by Bombardier Aerospace in Canada.

DASH 8 is a derivative produced by Bombardier Aerospace based on the DHC-8. It is a twin-engine turboprop airliner. The total number of aircraft produced is over 800.

Embraer E-Jet is a small jet airliner manufactured and marketed by Embraer, a Brazilian aircraft manufacturer. It is classified as a regional jet, the Embraer 195, the largest in the series, can be configured with up to 124 seats.
