about aviation

The airline industry provides services to transport passengers and cargo domestically and internationally by aircraft. The main operations in the airline industry are the transportation of passengers and cargo by airplanes, helicopters, and other equipment. In recent years, the airline industry has implemented various measures, such as increasing the number of slots for domestic flights and modifying runways and flight paths. In addition, the industry has been hit by the coronavirus in 2020, which has resulted in a significant decrease in the number of passengers traveling and a decline in duty-free store sales.
